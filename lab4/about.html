About the ITWS Program

Welcome to the Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute's Information Technology and Web Science program. Our program is designed to provide students with a solid foundation in information technology, web development, and related fields. We are committed to preparing the next generation of tech leaders with the skills and knowledge needed to excel in today's fast-paced digital world.

Key features of our ITWS program:

  • Comprehensive curriculum covering IT and web science topics
  • Experienced faculty members dedicated to your success
  • Hands-on projects and real-world experience
  • Networking opportunities with industry professionals
  • State-of-the-art facilities and resources
