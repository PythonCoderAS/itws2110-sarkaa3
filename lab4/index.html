Welcome to the ITWS Program

Explore our program and discover the exciting world of Information Technology and Web Science.

Cybersecurity

Explore advanced techniques in cybersecurity, including network security, cryptography, and ethical hacking. Learn to protect digital systems and data from cyber threats.

Data Science and Analytics

Dive into the world of data analytics, machine learning, and statistical modeling. Acquire skills in analyzing complex datasets, deriving meaningful insights, and making data-driven decisions.

Multidisciplinary Classes

Collaborate across disciplines with courses that integrate ITWS principles with other fields such as business, engineering, and social sciences. Develop a well-rounded skill set for diverse career opportunities.

Explore Diverse Focus Tracks

ITWS offers a multitude of specialized focus tracks, allowing students to tailor their education to their unique interests and career aspirations. Examples of focus tracks include:

  • Web Development
  • Human-Computer Interaction
  • Information Systems Management

Discover your passion within the expansive range of ITWS focus tracks and pave the way for a successful career in the digital world.
